about us

We help people build their dreams.

Bihar is progressing fast on the track of development, with unrelenting efforts being made towards strengthening infrastructure and promoting industrial growth. The government of Bihar is committed to improve competitiveness of the state's economy and achieving inclusive growth. Start-ups and Innovation play a key role in economic growth. This can be realized by promoting innovation and creating an ecosystem that nurtures start-ups from concept to commissioning. Apart from creating jobs, start-ups focus on innovation to create next gen solutions which bring economic dynamism.

our services

Core offerings that help startups connect, grow, and succeed

1. Startup Registration & Showcase We help Bihar-based startups register and showcase their business ideas, products, and services on our platform to reach investors, mentors, and partners.

2. Networking & Community Building Connect with like-minded founders, entrepreneurs, and innovators across Bihar through our community meetups, online forums, and startup circles.

3. Attend exclusive startup events, workshops, webinars, and coding hackathons to enhance your skills and network with key stakeholders in the ecosystem.

FAQ

+

What is Startup Connect Bihar?

It is a networking hub for Bihar-based startups to connect with mentors, investors, and fellow entrepreneurs while accessing events, resources, and funding opportunities.

+

Who can join this platform?

Any startup founder, entrepreneur, investor, mentor, or student from Bihar or interested in supporting the Bihar startup ecosystem can join.

+

How can I participate in startup events or webinars?

Visit the “Events” page for a list of upcoming events and click on “Register” for any event you’re interested in

+

Do you provide funding directly?

We don’t fund directly, but we connect startups with investors, government schemes, and funding partners.

+

Who runs this platform?

Startup Connect Bihar is a community-driven initiative supported by local entrepreneurs, volunteers, and ecosystem enablers in Bihar
